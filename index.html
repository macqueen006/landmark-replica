Popular Activities

On land or at sea, Landmark Leisure Beach is Packed with activities for friends and family.

AstroTurf Soccer

Play the beautiful game on a purpose built astroturf pitch which can accomodate anywhere from 5 to 8 a side. Organise mini leagues with friends and family with our astroturf league package.

Jet Skiing

Rent a jetski and enjoy an exhilirating ride across the Atlantic coastline with a backdrop of Hard Rock Cafe and the Eko Atlantic.

Basketball

Enjoy court bookings for corporate bodies, tournaments or those that don't have a team, you can show up suitably kitted and join like-mind for fun in alloted sessions.

Beach Paintball

A fast-paced, heart-racing activity of fun for friends and family. Manoeuver through obstacles while dodging and shooting paintballs at a whooping 190 mph at your opposing team members. Now how fun does that sound?

Beach Soccer

Enjoy matches with friends or host soccer tournaments on the sandy shores of the Landmark Leisure Beachfront. Beach soccer activity is made available on request.

Beach Volleyball

Landmark Leisure Beach is equipped with a well-cordoned area for beach volleyball activities open to everyone.

Cabanas & Loungers

With 20 cabanas and over 40 loungers along with beach kiosks operated by well-known international brands and high quality service, Landmark Leisure Beach accommodates a relaxed and serene atmosphere to make your escape from the busy city lifestyle possible.

Kite Surfing

Surf's up at the Landmark Leisure Beach! Experience gliding across the Atlantic coastline with conditions perfect for either kite or wind surfing.

The Landmark Leisure Beach, located at 3 & 4 Water Corporation Road - VI is Lagos’ first in-city premier private beachfront. The beach features a boardwalk along the Atlantic coastline, spanning the distance of the Landmark Village, home to Hardrock Café, Shiro Restaurant, the renowned Landmark Event Centre and the upcoming Retail Boulevard. It combines a myriad of leisure and recreational activities which caters to adults and children.
